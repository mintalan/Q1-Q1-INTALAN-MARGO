Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer is about an anime where there was a boy named Tanjiro and his whole family got killed by the demon lord named Muzan. Tanjiro's little sister, Nezuko, survived but became a demon. Tanjiro's goal from then on was to avenge his family, get stronger, and find a cure for his little sister.
